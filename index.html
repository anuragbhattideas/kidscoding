Who We Are

By day, I am a software engineers with a serious case of code-on-the-brain (15+ years' worth!). By night (and often, let's be honest, by day too), I am a dad. This project? It's our attempt to combine our two passions: building cool stuff with code, and helping kids learn cool stuff in general. Consider it a gift from a slightly sleep-deprived but enthusiastic parent/coder.

But it's more than just a fun project. I believe that in today's rapidly changing world, computer skills aren't just a "nice-to-have" â€“ they're essential. The future is digital, and kids who understand how technology works will have a huge advantage. We want to empower them to not just be consumers of technology, but creators and innovators.

Learning to code, problem-solve, and think critically about technology will open up a world of opportunities for them, no matter what path they choose in life. From coding the next groundbreaking app to simply navigating the digital world safely and effectively, these skills will be invaluable.

I am building this resource to help bridge the digital divide and make sure all kids have access to the tools they need to succeed in the 21st century and beyond. We hope it inspires a new generation of digital creators!
